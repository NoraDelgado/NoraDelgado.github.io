About me

I'm Nora Delgado, a student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Science with concentration in Data Science and Analytics. I possess a strong skillset in SQL, Tableau, and Excel. From a young age, my fascination with solving complex puzzles like Rubik's Cubes and Sudokus has shaped my approach to big data. As an aspiring Junior Data Analyst, I approach each data set as a multifaceted puzzle waiting to be solved. To me, uncovering hidden insights to solve problems is not just a professional challenge but a personal passion.
